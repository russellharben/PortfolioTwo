Hi, my name is Russell and I'm a web developer from Atlanta, GA. A couple years ago I began teaching myself to code in my free time and am currently enrolled in Georgia Tech's Full Stack Web Development and Programming Bootcamp.

In 2010 I graduated from the University of South Alabama with a BA in Communications and a minor in Business Management. After college I took a job as the Director of Marketing at a Top 40 radio station. I later took a job at a software development company in my hometown doing data analytics and account management.

A couple years later I moved to Florida and started a management position at an EZ-GO distributor, eventually moving to outside sales. It was a great experience. Still, coding was something I was always interested in learning but never had time to truly dedicate myself.

Eventually I took an opportunity to go back to work for the software company I had previously been with in Atlanta. This time I was afforded the opportunity to start spending time at work teaching myself the basics of web development and programming. I sharpened my skills just enough that I was able to land a few outside freelance projects building websites for two local businesses in Atlanta and a third for a logistics company in Richmond, Virginia.

These were outstanding learning experiences and ultimately led to opportunities at work to doing frontend design and coding. Our current clients, among others, include GPC, Target, Hunter Douglas, McKesson, JW Pepper, and more.
